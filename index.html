Located downhill from Kodaikanal, before the temple town of Palani, Oddanchatram is a sleep town acting as junction between them. This Hotel Grand Paradise is the only 3 star hotel in Oddanchatram. The staff is extremely courteous and efficient. The rooms are spacious and comfortable. The toilet and bathroom are clean and hygienic. There is a bar at the rear end of the hotel serving almost every type of alcoholic beverages. The stay offers complete value for money.
